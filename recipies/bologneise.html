Bologneise

Nat's What I Reckon - End of days bologneise

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 500g beef / pork mince
  • glass red wind
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup stock
  • herbs

Steps

  1. Dice onion and carrot
  2. Stir fry in pan with olive oil
  3. Add mince and fry the shit out of it
  4. Add rosemary / other herbs
  5. Cook off any liquid and add wine then tomato paste
  6. Add milk and then stock
  7. Season with salt and pepper, add bay leaf
  8. Cook for 45 minutes or more
