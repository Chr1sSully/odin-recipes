Chicken Curry

Classic white

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Spice paste
  • 1 Can chopped tomatoes
  • 100g Greek yoghurt

Steps

  1. Cook onion, salt then add garlic and ginger
  2. Add chikcen chunks then spice paste and tomatoes & 1 cup water
  3. Simmer for 30 mins
